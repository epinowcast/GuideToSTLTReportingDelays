# Manuscript

Guidance manuscript for accounting for reporting delays in STLT surveillance
data.

## Aim

Provide practical guidance for data collectors and modellers working with
reporting delays in state, tribal, local, and territorial public health surveillance
systems.

## Target audiences

- Epidemiologists and public health practitioners implementing nowcasting
- Modellers expanding their understanding of nowcasting in practice

## Outputs

- Preprint submission
- Journal submission (target: epidemiology journal)

## Files

- `paper.qmd` - Main manuscript in Quarto format
